Our Mission

Unscramble Words Pro was built with one goal: to be the fastest and most comprehensive word-finding tool available online. Whether you're a competitive Scrabble player preparing for a tournament, a casual Words With Friends enthusiast, or someone solving the daily Wordle, our tool gives you instant access to every valid word hiding in your letters.

What Makes Us Different

  • Comprehensive dictionaries — We combine the ENABLE word list (standard for North American word games), Collins SOWPODS (used in UK, Australian, and international Scrabble), and the dwyl English corpus for maximum coverage of over 370,000 words.
  • Instant results — Our engine processes your letters and returns all valid words in milliseconds, with Scrabble scores calculated in real time. Try it with letters like CRANE, HEART, or STRANGE.
  • Regional awareness — We automatically detect your region and suggest the appropriate dictionary (TWL for US/Canada, SOWPODS for UK/Australia) so your results are relevant to your game.
  • Privacy-first — All your preferences, favourites, and recent searches stay in your browser. We don't store personal data on our servers.
  • Works offline — Once the dictionary is cached in your browser, the tool works even without an internet connection.

Our Dictionaries

Accuracy matters in word games. That's why we source our word lists from the most respected authorities:

  • TWL (Tournament Word List) — The official dictionary for Scrabble tournaments in the United States and Canada, maintained by NASPA (North American Scrabble Players Association). Contains approximately 187,000 words.
  • Collins SOWPODS / CSW — The official dictionary for Scrabble tournaments in the United Kingdom, Australia, and all international events sanctioned by WESPA. Contains approximately 280,000 words.
  • ENABLE — The Enhanced North American Benchmark Lexicon, a widely used open-source word list that forms the basis for many word game applications.
