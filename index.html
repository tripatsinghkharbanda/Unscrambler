Scrabble in the US & Canada (TWL)
North American tournaments use the TWL (~187k words) maintained by NASPA. Our tool includes the full list. Popular clubs meet in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, and more.
Scrabble in the UK & International (SOWPODS / Collins)
Collins Scrabble Words (~280k entries) is the standard for UK and WESPA events. It includes British spellings like colour, favour, and centre. Major events span London, Manchester, Edinburgh, and beyond. The ABSP organises UK nationals.
Scrabble in Canada
Canadian players use the TWL but also accept British-leaning spellings (colour, favourite) that appear in the TWL. Championships attract players from Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, Calgary, and Ottawa.
Scrabble in Australia & New Zealand
ASPA and Scrabble Australia use Collins CSW. Australian English follows British conventions plus unique words from the Macquarie Dictionary. Events are held in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, and more.

Strategy Tips

Opening Moves & Rack Management
Open through the centre star with a 5-letter word (CRANE, SLATE, TRACE) for 20–40 pts. Keep 3–4 consonants and 2–3 vowels. Exchange tiles rather than play a weak word that wrecks your rack.
Parallel Plays, Hooks & Bingos
Parallel plays create multiple two-letter words for 30–60 pts. Hooks add one letter to an existing word. Bingos use all 7 tiles for a 50-pt bonus — study 7- and 8-letter words to boost frequency.
Endgame Strategy
Count remaining tiles, track power letters, and aim to go out first. Save an S or blank for flexibility. The player who empties their rack gains the opponent’s remaining tile values.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word unscrambler work?

Can I use this for official Scrabble?
Our dictionary includes all words from the TWL (Tournament Word List) used in US and Canadian Scrabble, plus the full Collins Scrabble Words (CSW/SOWPODS) used in UK, Australian, and international tournaments. Every word shown is valid in at least one official Scrabble dictionary.
What is the difference between TWL and SOWPODS?
TWL (Tournament Word List) is the official dictionary for Scrabble in the United States and Canada, maintained by NASPA. SOWPODS (now called Collins Scrabble Words / CSW) is used in the UK, Australia, and all WESPA international events. SOWPODS contains approximately 280,000 words versus TWL's 187,000 — the extra words include many British, Australian, and international English spellings.
Is QI a valid Scrabble word?
Yes. QI (meaning "vital force" in Chinese philosophy) is valid in both TWL and SOWPODS. It scores 11 points and is one of the most important two-letter words to know. It allows you to play Q without needing U on your rack.
What are the best two-letter Scrabble words?
The highest-scoring two-letter words are QI (11), ZA (11), ZO (11, SOWPODS only), XI (9), XU (9), JO (9), AX (9), EX (9), OX (9), KA (6), and KI (6). Learning all valid two-letter words is considered the single most impactful study technique for improving at Scrabble.
Does this work for Words With Friends?
Yes. While Words With Friends uses its own proprietary dictionary and a slightly different scoring system, our 370,000+ word list covers the vast majority of WWF-valid words. Use it to find playable words, then verify in the game if needed.
Can I use this as a Wordle helper?
Absolutely. Enter confirmed letters, set the word length to 5, and use the "Starts with", "Ends with", or "Contains" filters to narrow results based on your Wordle clues. This reveals all possible five-letter answers matching your known information.
Does the tool work offline?
After the first visit, the full 370,000-word dictionary is cached in your browser's IndexedDB storage. Subsequent visits load the dictionary from cache, so the tool works even without an internet connection.
How do I find the highest-scoring word?
Sort your results by "Score ↓" using the dropdown menu. The top results will be the highest-scoring words. Words highlighted in green are the highest-value words in the current result set.
What is a bingo in Scrabble?
A bingo (or bonus) occurs when you use all seven tiles from your rack in a single play. This earns a 50-point bonus on top of the word's base score. Common bingo-length words are seven or eight letters. Use our tool to practise finding seven-letter words from any rack.
Are proper nouns valid in Scrabble?
No. Proper nouns (names of people, places, brands) are not allowed in Scrabble. Our dictionary excludes proper nouns, so every word shown in your results is a valid common English word playable in Scrabble.
How many words are in the Scrabble dictionary?
The TWL (US/Canada) contains approximately 187,000 words. The Collins Scrabble Words / SOWPODS (UK/International) contains approximately 280,000 words. Our tool combines both plus additional valid English words for a total of over 370,000 searchable entries.
